Listening, reading, hand-writing and speaking, all real natural stuff in any learning really. Obviously, they are obvious steps in processing a language. But how about typing on a computer? Even typing out that tiny japanese paragraph in simple hiragana and katakana took forever. Because there's a million tiny things that stops you in your tracks and stumps you. How do I jump between keyboard layouts and alphabets? How do I type out びっくり instead of びつくり? How do I type out a question mark when on the Japanese keyboard layout? Why is every single keyboard shortcut different now?

Switching between keyboard layouts and learning all of these shortcut commands is making it feel like I am a new born baby. I pride myself in my computer knowledge yet now it's useless.
